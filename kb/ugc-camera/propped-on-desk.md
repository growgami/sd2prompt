# Propped-on-Desk Camera

Office / productivity shots where the phone is laying on a surface filming upward.

## Key Insight

Describe the physical position of the smartphone, not the scene from an external observer's perspective.

## Tested Prompt Pattern

```
smartphone laying flat on desk next to keyboard filming upward,
extreme low angle from desk surface level, three-quarter profile,
60cm distance, desk surface and keyboard edge visible at bottom of frame
```

## What Makes It Work

- Low angle from surface level (not "looking up at person")
- Foreground objects partially cut off by frame edge (keyboard edge, pen, coffee cup)
- Realistic distance: 40-60cm
- Off-center composition: real propped phones are always off-center

## What to Avoid

- Centered/symmetrical compositions (looks CG, not phone footage)
- Describing from third person ("camera on desk films person") instead of describing the phone's physical position
