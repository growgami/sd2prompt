# Selfie-with-Arm (Front Camera POV)

Creator filming herself with extended arm, looking into lens.

## Key Insight

Describe the result the camera captures, not the filming method.

## Tested Prompt Pattern

```
filming herself with right arm extended toward camera,
she looks directly into lens
```

## What Makes It Work

- Specify arm direction (right/left arm extended)
- Eye contact with lens (not "looking at camera")
- Describe what's visible in the background behind the subject

## What to Avoid

- Never use just "selfie" alone. Produces a third-person meta-selfie: someone photographing themselves seen from the outside
- "Taking a selfie" = third person. "Filming herself with arm extended toward camera" = first person POV
