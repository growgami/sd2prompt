# Fit Check (Street Level Phone)

Phone propped at street level filming upward. Subject steps back into frame and poses.

## Tested Prompt Pattern

```
phone mounted on a glass surface at street level filming upward,
she steps back from camera into frame on a wide sidewalk,
[specific outfit description],
does a confident pose showing her outfit, one hand on hip, slight turn,
pedestrians walking past
```

## What Makes It Work

- Full body must be visible (that's the point of a fit check)
- Give 2.5-3s minimum for the step-back-and-pose
- Specific outfit description is critical (Seedance needs to know what to render)
- Background activity (pedestrians) adds realism

## What to Avoid

- Cropping above the knees (defeats the purpose)
- Vague outfit description ("nice clothes" vs "oversized beige knit sweater and black leggings")
