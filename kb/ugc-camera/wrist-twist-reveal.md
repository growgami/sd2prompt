# Wrist-Twist POV Reveal

Common UGC move: creator films herself then twists wrist to show the view/food/location/product.

## Tested Prompt Pattern

```
she films herself smiling then twists her wrist rotating the camera
180 degrees to reveal [what's being shown]
```

## What Makes It Work

- Must be within a continuous shot segment (no scene cut between selfie and reveal)
- Give at least 2.5-3s for the rotation to feel natural
- The reveal target should be described specifically

## What to Avoid

- Splitting the selfie and reveal into separate timeline segments (breaks the continuous motion)
- Giving less than 2s for the twist (looks jarring)
